The Song Series

Welcome.

This is a small collection of web projects exploring the mobile first strategy and responsive design.

To the left you will find links to three webpages. I built them with the intention of practising grid layouts and the practice of writing clean code.

These three projects are each based on and inspired by a song. My goal was to create a design for each one that evokes the atmosphere and spirit of the chosen song as much as possible. In all three I used the lyrics as a key feature. I also included pictures which complement the mood of the song as well as relevant information about the album and artist in question.

I hope you have a look at my projects and enjoy what I have created.

Thank you.
